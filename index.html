About Me

I am a MS student of Robotics Department at University of Michigan (UMich), where I work on learning-based robot control at the ROAHM lab with professor Ram Vasudevan and Dr. Elena Shrestha. I have also collaborated with Dr. Howie Choset and Dr. Matthew Travers during my time at the Biorobotics lab at Carnegie Mellon University as an intern. Prior to that, I received my bachelor degree from Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS Pilani), majoring in Mechanical Engineering, with a minor in Robotics and Automation. Advised by professor Arshad Javed, I had a great research experience on classical control of robot manipulators, and learning-based control of UAVs.

I am particularly interested in robot learning and scene understanding, and proficient in developing robotic algorithms. When I'm not immersed in technology, you can find me playing lawn tennis, and watching movies.
